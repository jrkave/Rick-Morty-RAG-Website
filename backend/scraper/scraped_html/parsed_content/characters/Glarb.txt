 Glarb is a field reporter for Glub 7 News. She covered the arrest of Rick (C-132) and Morty (C-132) after they had a twelve-hour standoff with the Time Police. She was unable to get a comment from Detective Tock or Jerry (C-132) Appearance: Glarb is a green, three-eyed alien with an upturned nose, long ears, protrusions on her neck, and tentacles for arms. She has grey hair and a few wrinkles on her face, giving her a middle-aged appearance.
