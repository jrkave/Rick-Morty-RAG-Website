 Amber Bunks, formerly known as Amber Maps, was a citizen of 41-Kepler B. Biography:: Amber studied in the same school as Fred Bunks, and they became lovers. One day, Fred moved to a distant place so he could enroll in college. She and Fred remained in an unstable long-distance relationship. The two broke up after Fred admitted to having hooked up with someone else. He tried talking to her in person, but Amber refused to listen. At some point, Amber married Lawrence Maps and changed her surname to Maps. The two had one son and one daughter. In her middle age, she was contacted by Fred through social media. After meeting at a restaurant, the two fell in love again, and Amber later divorced Lawrence so she could marry Fred. The two lived the rest of their days happily married, and Amber was the first one to pass away.
