Ricardo Montoya is one of the alternate versions of Rick Sanchez who appears in "Gotron Jerrysis Rickvangelion". Biography:: He, along with other versions of the Smith family, was invited by Rick C-137 on a cook-off to discuss the repairment of their GoTrons. Appearance: Ricardo Montoya wears a white jacket with a red shirt and white jeans. He also has a scruffy haircut instead of a spiky one that is a darker shade of blue which is balding like with most Ricks, and a scar on his right cheek. Trivia: Ricardo Montoya is a parody of Al Pacino's portrayal of Antonio "Tony" Montana from the 1983 crime drama film Scarface.
