Holly Hooks is a student at Harry Herpson High School that is most likely around the same age as Jessica. She is often seen talking with Jessica and any other friends that are around, such as Grace or Tricia. Her voice actress is Tara Strong. The original Holly Hooks presumably became a Cronenberg in the Cronenberged Dimension.
