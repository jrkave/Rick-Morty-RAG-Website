Jerry Smith (Evil Rick's Target Dimension) is an alternate version of the original Jerry, who appeared in the episode Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind, he is the husband of Beth Smith, the father of Summer Smith and Morty Smith, and the son-in-law of Rick Sanchez and Mrs. Sanchez, he is also the son of Leonard Smith and Joyce Smith, and the nephew of the Unnamed Uncle. Biography:: In the episode Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind, Jerry is seen sitting at the table, eating breakfast with his family, when all of a sudden Evil Rick and Evil Morty appeared from a portal in the room. Evil Rick and Evil Morty shoot his father-in-law Rick and tranquilize and kidnap his son Morty, leaving Jerry screaming in terror and asking what was going on, along with his wife and daughter. Personality: Jerry always tries to think of the best interest of the family, but his attempt to be the patriarch of the family can often be misguided by his self-centered nature. This causes him a great deal of conflict with Rick as his father-in-law clearly has no respect for him whatsoever. Apperances: "Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind" "Unmortricken" (flashback cameo)
