Concerto is a minor, one-time villain, who only appeared in the ending credits of "Pickle Rick" as well as in the theme song for Season 3. He is a music-related villain, who attempted to crush Rick and Morty with the hammers on a giant piano. He was killed by Jaguar before he could do any harm to Rick and Morty. He also killed 3 other people. He has piano teeth and piano hammer elbows.
