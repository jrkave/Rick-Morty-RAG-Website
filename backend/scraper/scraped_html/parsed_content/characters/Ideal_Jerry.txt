Ideal Jerry is one of the mythologs of Jerry Smith. He appeared in the episode Big Trouble In Little Sanchez. He is the way Beth views Jerry when he is fighting off Jerry's Mytholog. Appearance: The ideal Jerry in Beth's eyes, is a taller, selfless, more muscular version of him with a thick belt buckle around his waist.
