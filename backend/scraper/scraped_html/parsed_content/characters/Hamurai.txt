Hamurai was a false character, portrayed by the Memory Parasites. He was mentioned to have gone to college with Reverse Giraffe. Biography:: Hamurai was introduced to the group after the parasites made Rick remember the barbecue, debuting alongside tons of other characters. Much later, the Smith family discovered that the parasites can only create pleasant memories. Hamurai was last seen worried when the family decided to kill the parasites. He was killed off-screen as the Smiths and Mr. Poopybutthole were the last survivors. Appearance: Hamurai is a samurai who wears a suit of armor made out of meat.
