Jerry's school friend is a friend of Jerry Smith (5126). Biography:: He first appears in the episode "Bethic Twinstinct" when teenager Jerry dreams of Beth. He warns Jerry that Beth is a dangerous person.  In the episode "The Jerrick Trap", he admires the space shuttle on a school field trip. Episode appearances: "Bethic Twinstinct" (flashback) "The Jerrick Trap" (flashback) Trivia: He is the first character from Dimension 5126 whose alternate versions were not shown in Dimension C-137, Prime Dimension, Dimension C-131 and Parmesan Dimension.
