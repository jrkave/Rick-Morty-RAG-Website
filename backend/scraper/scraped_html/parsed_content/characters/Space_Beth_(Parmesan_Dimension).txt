Space Beth Smith of the Parmesan Dimension is the original Space Beth that originated from the Parmesan Dimension. Biography:: Space Beth, along with the rest of the Smith family, managed to save their dimension from Mr. Frundles before dying from "natural causes". The titular Smith family bury their bodies in the backyard and continue to live in this dimension.  She, along with the rest of the Smith family from this dimension, appear to be decaying, as if they have been dead for quite some time. Appearances: "Solaricks"  Trivia: This is the only variant of Space Beth we've seen in the series outside of Dimension C-131.
