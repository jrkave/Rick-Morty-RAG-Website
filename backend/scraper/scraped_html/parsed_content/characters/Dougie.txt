Dougie is the father of Mordi and friend of Reek. Biography:: He appears in the "Bush Dimension" where he attempts to cure Mordi of a snake bite by putting a Witchetty Grub on his genitals. This turns out to be a prank though and he helps them reach Uncle Barry through a portal he summons on the ground.
