 Jerry Jr. is the Meta-Son of Summer Smith (C-131). He appears in a fake "previously on" montage created by Previous Leon, and doesn't actually exist as a person. Biography:: He is first mentioned at the beginning of the episode "Full Meta Jackrick", at Summer's birthday party, when Summer announced her pregnancy. Later at Jerry's funeral, Summer cries and says that she will name her child "Jerry Junior." Jerry Jr. first appears at Beth's execution. Summer holds him in her arms.  Appearance: Jerry Jr. looks like a little one-year-old baby. His face shape is similar to that of his mother Summer and grandfather Jerry, but his nose is similar to Uncle Morty's. Episode appearances: "Full Meta Jackrick" Trivia: If he really existed, he would be Summer's second son after Naruto.
