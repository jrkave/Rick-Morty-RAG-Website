Slow Mobius's son is the son of Slow Mobius and Slow Mobius's wife. He has a sister and a step-brother. Biography:: Slow Mobius's son first appears on one of the holographic screens when Rick Prime decides to kill Slow Mobius in all realities. He (or an alternate version of him) was shocked by the sudden disappearance of his father. Most likely, he forgot his real father, just like his sister.  When Slow Mobius's wife and the Unnamed Green Alien began to live together, Slow Mobius's son and Slow Mobius's daughter were able to become friends with the Unnamed Green Alien's son.  Appearances: "Unmortricken"
