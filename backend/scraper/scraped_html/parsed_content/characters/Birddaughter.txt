Birddaughter is the daughter of Birdperson and Tammy Guterman. Biography:: Not much is known about her, other than that she was raised by the Galactic Federation in prison because she was the daughter of Birdperson, a wanted terrorist, and Tammy, an undercover Federation agent. She appears in the post-credits scene of "Rickternal Friendshine of the Spotless Mort" wherein another prison inmate picks on her before shoving her food on the floor. Birddaughter then beats up the inmate before being dragged away by two Gromflomites. She appears again in "How Poopy Got His Poop Back", in which it is revealed her resurrected father liberated her from prison. She is now living with Birdperson, her hair dyed black, and adopting an emo appearance. She gets annoyed by Birdperson going through her things, even expressing how much she misses living in the prison and storms off. At the end of the episode, her father learns she is trying to break into a Federation facility and goes off to stop her.
