Crystallized Morty is a version of Morty Smith who has been crystalized. He is briefly seen in a hospital in The Ricklantis Mixup. Biography:: Crystallized Morty is shown on the CN news coverage of President Morty's visit to a hospital on the Citadel and greeting injured Mortys. This Morty is hospitalized due to being trapped in a crystal, most likely by his Rick. He is greeted by President Morty, but unable to shake his hand, so President Morty merely lays his hand on the crystal. He shares a room with Trunk Morty, who shook hands with President Morty before him. Appearance: Crystallized Morty dresses in a damaged yellow shirt and blue pants.
