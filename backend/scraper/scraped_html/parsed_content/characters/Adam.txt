Adam is a Narnian appearing in the episode Mort Dinner Rick Andre as the main antagonist alongside his ancestors and descendants. Biography:: Adam was a young child before being forced into training by his Mother, he fought several holograms of Morty until he was ready, he was sent to the Parmesan dimension through a portal to kill Morty, while trying to kill him, he shoots a hole through the wall assassinating Mr. Nimbus's octopus and looking confused, Jessica later stabs a tube connecting to Adam. He see's a hallucination of his Mother as a type of bird before subsequently being pulled back into Narnia and dying.
