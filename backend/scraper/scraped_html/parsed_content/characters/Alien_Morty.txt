Alien Morty is an alternate version of Morty Smith who appeared in the episode Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind. Appearance: Alien Morty is an alien teenage boy. He has slimy, lime-green skin. He has two curly antennae on his head and short brown hair. He has three slightly squinty eyes, with one of them on his forehead and he also has a big nose.
