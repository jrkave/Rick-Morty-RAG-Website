 The Pope is an antagonist who appears in the episode Mort: Ragnarick.  Biography:: Upon finding Bigfoot, he sees him as a creature outside of God's domain, due to him being a sentient mythical beast. The Pope uses him to kill countless people. The Pope is given infinite power from Valhalla, and uses it to kill Rick, Morty, and Bigfoot several times. Rick eventually defeats him by trapping him in a Pokemon ball. In the Post-Credit Scene, Rick is seen taking part in an illegal Pokemon battle using the Pope to battle another trainer when the FBI arrives and shuts the place down while Rick escapes via portal. After freeing him, the Pope asks who's running the Vatican to which the FBI shows a video of the Rick Clone as the new pope much to his horror.
