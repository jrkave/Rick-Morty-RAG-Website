Artist Morty is an alternate version of Morty Smith that appeared in the episode Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind. He is a version of Morty who is a French artist who draws sketches in his notebook. His fate was unknown as of the events of "The Rickshank Rickdemption." but was revealed that he survived the Citadel collision in the episode, "The Ricklantis Mixup." Appearance: Artist Morty is a young Caucasian teenage boy with brown hair and a yellow long sleeved shirt, blue pants, and white shoes. He wears a dark red beret and he also has a brown mustache.
