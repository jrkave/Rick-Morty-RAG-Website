This unnamed green alien is the widow of an Green Alien's wife until she was presumably murdered. He is now the husband of Slow Mobius's wife and has a son, an step-daughter and a step-son. Biography:: He first appears on one of the planets, in a bar. He met Slow Mobius's wife, who revealed that she had also lost her spouse, and they were drinking together. Eventually, they moved in together as romantic partners, living happily with their children while still keeping the memory of their former partners. Appearances: "Unmortricken"
