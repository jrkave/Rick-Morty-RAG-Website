The Tumblorkians are a blobby, four-eyed slime alien race that exist in the world of Rick and Morty. According to Shrimply Pibbles the Tumblorkians have acid for blood. At one point a huge group of Tumblorkians held a protest with Shrimply Pibbles. They are known to inhabit planet Plopstar. Appearances: "Mortynight Run" "The Wedding Squanchers" "Vindicators 3: The Return of Worldender" Trivia: The name might be taken from the website "Tumblr".
