 Marta is a character in Roy: A Life Well Lived that is controlled by a fragment of Morty and was separated from him via the events of Rick: A Mort Well Lived. She could have been a normal NPC in previous games of Roy although her birth may also have been influenced by Morty controlling her parents for the first several decades of the game. Appearance: Marta starts off as an average black teenage girl with curly hair. She later cuts her hair to look like Morty's.
