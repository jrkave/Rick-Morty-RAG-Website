Morty Smith of the Parmesan Dimension is an alternate version of the original Morty that appeared in Solaricks. Biography:: At some point in Solaricks, Morty, along with his entire family, managed to save their dimension from Mr. Frundles before dying of "natural causes". Shortly after, the titular Smith Family arrives to the Parmesan Dimension and bury the corpses of the native Smith Family. They then live out their lives in their places. He, along with the rest of the Smith family from this dimension, appear to be decaying, as if they have been dead for quite some time. Appearances: "Solaricks"
