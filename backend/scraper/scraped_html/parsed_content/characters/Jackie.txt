 Jackie is a Gazorpian who lives in the underground city of Paradise on the planet Gazorpazorp. Her friend Veronica Ann Bennet was sentenced to "the silent treatment" for her bangs, so Jackie started to completely ignore her, like the rest of the female gazorpians.  Biography:: Jackie was present at the gazorpian court when her friend Veronica was put on trial because of her bad bangs. Veronica was sentenced to be ignored by all other gazorpians, which Jackie quickly followed. She was also present at the trial of Rick Sanchez and Summer Smith before they were let go, and sent to leave Gazorpazorp as soon as possible.
