 Jerry Smith of the Parmesan Dimension is the original Jerry that originated from the Parmesan Dimension.  Biography:: He, along with the rest of the Smith family, managed to save their dimension from Mr. Frundles before dying from "natural causes". The titular Smith family bury their bodies in the backyard and continue to live in this dimension.
