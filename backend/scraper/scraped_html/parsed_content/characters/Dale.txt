Dale was a giant that appeared in the episode "Meeseeks and Destroy", he was also the husband of Rose, and the father of their unnamed son. Appearance: Dale was fat and muscular giant, he had dark brown hair and is overweight, he wearing a brown fur kilt around his waist. Biography:: When Rick Sanchez and Morty Smith entered in his house, Dale came into the kitchen and suspected something. As he walked he slipped, and bashed his head on the corner of a table, killing him. When his wife Rose with their son came in, the woman saw her husband's lifeless body and subsequently accused the pair of murder.  Trivia: According to The Art of Rick and Morty, Dale was designed after Dan Harmon.
