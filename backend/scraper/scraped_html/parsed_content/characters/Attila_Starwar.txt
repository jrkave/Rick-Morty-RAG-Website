Attila Starwar is the main protagonist of Ball Fondlers, and a recurring character in Rick and Morty. He is the leader of the team. Appearance: Attila is a large, muscular, African-American man. He has a beard and styles his hair into two Afro pigtails. He wears army fatigue pants, boots, and a golden army necklace around his neck that lays against his bare chest. On his wrists he has a golden wristband, and bracelets. Appearances: "Rixty Minutes" "Mortynight Run" "The Ricks Must Be Crazy" Rick and Morty Issue 6 Trivia: He is a parody on B. A.
