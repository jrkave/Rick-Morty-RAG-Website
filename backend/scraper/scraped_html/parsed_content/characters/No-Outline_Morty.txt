No-Outline Morty (also known as Lineless Morty) is a alternate version of Morty Smith who is only seen as a background character in The Creepy Morty where Waiter Morty serves him a drink. Appearance: He looks exactly like Morty, except he has a slightly crudely drawn design, no outlines even thought his nose and mouth has a black outline. Trivia: Most people believe that he is a South Park-style version of Morty but it was never confirmed.
