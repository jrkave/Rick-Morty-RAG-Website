Fran Dreslicar was a planet heisted by Heistotron along with Earth and many other planets. Fran Dreslicar is a very large purple-ish brown gas planet with a thin ring of rock and debris encircling the planet, giving it a look similar to a mix between Jupiter and Saturn. History: Fran Dreslicar, like a number of other planets, was stolen by the heist robot Heistotron built by Rick Sanchez. It was kept next to Mars and was destroyed when the space station blew up thanks to Rick Sanchez. Trivia: The planet is likely named after the actress Fran Drescher.
