Timbus was green cloudy planet with a ring system orbiting the it. It was destroyed in the explosion that took out Heistotron's space station. History: Timbus was stolen by Heistotron the heist robot, and placed next to Flump in a chamber that kept it suspended in place. Rick Sanchez and Morty Smith managed to destroy Heistotron and Randotron, resulting in the space station self destructing with all the planets inside, except for Earth.
