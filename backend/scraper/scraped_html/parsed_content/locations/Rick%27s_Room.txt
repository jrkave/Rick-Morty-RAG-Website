Rick's Room is the bedroom of Rick Sanchez. His room makes its first appearance in the episode, Close Rick-counters of the Rick Kind.  Appearance: Rick's room is a small bedroom with grey walls and beige floor. His bed is on the side facing to the radio on the shelf next to the sink, the green carpet and papers, the brown drawer on the corner with a coat hanger next to the brown shelf, and boxes. Episode Appearances: "Close Rick-counters of the Rick Kind" (post-credits) "The Old Man and the Seat" "Rickmurai Jack" (flashback) "Night Family"
