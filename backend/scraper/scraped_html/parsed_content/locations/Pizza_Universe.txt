 The Pizza Universe is a dimension where Pizza and Humans are in the opposite roles than they are in the real world. Description: The inhabitants are anthropomorphized slices of pizza with arms, legs, eyes and mouths and pizza is made out of human beings. Different races of people are the different toppings. The three known toppings so far are Caucasian, African American and Hispanic people. They show an affinity for eating humans and even display preferences for different ethnicities. History: In Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind Rick and Morty briefly passed through this dimension when they were being pursued by the Council of Ricks. Appearances: Close Rick-Counters of the Rick Kind Trivia: Pizza Morty is said to be from this dimension.
