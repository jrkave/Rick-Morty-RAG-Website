 Time Court is an intergalactic court that deals with crimes that involve tampering with time. They utilize the Time Police to enforce their laws and capture criminals. History: Rick and Morty of Dimension C-132 were brought before the Time Court for dimensional tampering, theft, collusion, destruction of parallel realities, assault with deadly weapon and resisting arrest. Rick attempted soliciting sex to one of the judges, which was then added to their crimes. Time Court ruled that Rick and morty were to spend their remaining days in the Clackspire Labyrinth.
