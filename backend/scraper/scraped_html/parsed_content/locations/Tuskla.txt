Tuskla is an electric car company founded and owned by Elon Tusk. It produces electric cars with tusks on the front. History: Elon Tusk founded the company Tuskla and started up a factory to produce his cars. He set up an office at the factory, where he would conduct business and acknowledge the input and effort from all of his workers. Elon was visited one day at his office by Rick Sanchez and Morty Smith, who asked for his assistance with taking down Heistotron.  Trivia: Tuskla is a parody of the real electric car company, Tesla. All employees wear a red body suit and protective goggles.
