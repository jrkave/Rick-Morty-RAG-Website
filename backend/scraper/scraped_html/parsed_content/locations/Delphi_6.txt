Delphi 6 is a planet located somewhere in the universe, first mentioned by Alan Rails in the episode "Vindicators 3: The Return of Worldender".  History: At some point near the end of Alan Rails and Supernova's marriage; she and Million Ants were stranded on this planet for 3 days. Being stranded turned out to be a cover for their affair during the 3 days on Delphi 6. Alan Rails says he could sense Million Ants' and Supernova's chemistry had changed during their stay.
