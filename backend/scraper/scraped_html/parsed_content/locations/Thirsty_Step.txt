 The Thirsy Step is a tavern/pub that is seen in the episode Meeseeks and Destroy. Rick and Morty encounter Mr. Jellybean at this tavern. This is also the first time that the Shmeckel currency is used when the waitress says to other patrons that her boob job cost 25 Shmeckels. Mr. Booby Buyer appears to be a frequent here.
