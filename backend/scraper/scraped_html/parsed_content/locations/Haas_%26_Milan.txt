Haas & Milan is the advertising company that Jerry used to work at until he got fired in its debut episode, "M. Night Shaym-Aliens!". After being maliciously acquired by an intergalactic holding company, the once-independent shop installed Kl’iiik Mijupsrit, a Grunglorkian, as their new CEO.
