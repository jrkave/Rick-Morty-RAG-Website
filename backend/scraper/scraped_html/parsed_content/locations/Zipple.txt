Zipple was a reddish-brown planet heisted by Heistotron and later destroyed when the robot's space station self-destructed. History: Zippy was only one of a number of planets heisted by Heistotron, the heist robot made by Rick Sanchez. The whole planet blew up in the explosion that followed after Rick caused both Heistotron and Randotron to malfunction, and somehow setting the space station to self destruct. Trivia: The planet wears a bandana in a similar fashion to a number of rap artists have done, most notably Tupac Shakur.
