 The Resort planet is an unnamed planet covered with forests and wild nature. It is the home of the Brosephamons and where the Immortality Field Resort is located. An artificial black hole is located close to the planet and is used by a cruiseliner to reach the resort. History: The resort planet was the home of two warring alien species before Rick intervened and provided one side with highly advanced weaponry, easily defeating the Brosephamons.
