 Shoney's is a restaurant appearing in "Star Mort Rickturn of the Jerri" that was previously used to represent Rick Sanchez's cerebellum in "The Rickshank Rickdemption." It appears as an easter egg on supervisor Rick's coffee mug in "The Ricklantis Mixup." Episode Appearances: "The Rickshank Rickdemption" "Pickle Rick" (mentioned) "The Ricklantis Mixup" (pictured) "Star Mort Rickturn of the Jerri" "Night Family" (mentioned) Trivia: Shoney's is the name of a real life restaurant chain in the US.
