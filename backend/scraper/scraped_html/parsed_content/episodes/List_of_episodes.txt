Rick and Morty is an American animated television series which premiered on December 2, 2013, on Cartoon Network's late-night programming block Adult Swim. As of December 17th 2023, 71 episodes of Rick and Morty have aired. In May 2018, it was announced that the series was renewed for 70 more episodes, which renewed the series to a tenth season.
