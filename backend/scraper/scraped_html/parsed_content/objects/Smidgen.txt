The smidgen is a currency used in some dimensions. It comes in the form of purple bills and is first shown in the episode "Raising Gazorpazorp". Rick uses this currency to purchase a Multi-phase Quantum Resonator and a Gazorpian sex robot at an alien pawn shop. Appearances: "Raising Gazorpazorp" Trivia: The name of the currency comes from the word smidgen, which is a small amount of something.
