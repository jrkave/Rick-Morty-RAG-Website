Jerry's Mind Blowers is a device that allows the user to see the memories of Jerry Smith. History: Jerry was looking through the garage for his putter, when he found Jerry's Mind Blowers inside of a box. The viewer (and Jerry) then proceed to see a parody of E.T. Appearance: The device looks similar to a helmet with goggles, except that there is two big wires in the back, some added technology on the sides of the helmet, and a slot in the helmet to insert a desired memory VHS.
