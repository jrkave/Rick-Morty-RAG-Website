 CN is a news station on the Citadel of Ricks. Rick D716 and Rick D716-B are co-anchors for CN and Rick D716-C is a reporter. CN first made an appearance in The Ricklantis Mixup. CN airs a program in the morning called Citadel Morning News. The name CN appears to be taken from CNN, and likely stands for Citadel News. Trivia: CN is a parody of Cartoon Network (abbreviated as CN), where they broadcast the series «Rick and Morty», the series itself is in the multiverse Cartoon Network.
