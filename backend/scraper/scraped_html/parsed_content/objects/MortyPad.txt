The MortyPad is a device made by Rick to track if there are any missing Mortys who lost their Ricks. It is a useful device whenever, wherever, and whatever. The MortyPad is used to look at your Mortys stats, the MortyDeck, your Items, Quests, to contact Salesman Rick if you want to buy useful supplies, Settings, and Google Play or Game Center to check your Achievements you accomplished while in-game.
