 The Cure for Tuberculosis was a creation of Rick's. It was nearly used to cure Ruben of his ailment after the disease was freed inside of Anatomy Park. Unfortunately, Ruben died before Rick could administer it to him. Trivia: Tuberculosis is a difficult illness to treat, but Rick nonchalantly pulled a cure from his lab coat.
