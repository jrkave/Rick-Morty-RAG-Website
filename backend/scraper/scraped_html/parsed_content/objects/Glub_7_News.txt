Glub 7 News is a news network featured in Rick and Morty Issue 1. It uses provocative headlines and focuses on stories that are similar to those that would happen on Earth, which they are aware of and make a point of mentioning. History: Glub 7 News was present on Earth to report on the arrest of Rick and Morty after a 12 hour standoff with the Time Police. They were unable to reach Detective Tock or Jerry Smith for comments on the issue.  Employees: Glarb - news reporter Fleeblebox IV - news anchorman Flex Xando - sports reporter
