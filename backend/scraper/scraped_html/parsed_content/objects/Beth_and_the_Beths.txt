 Beth and the Beths is a hard rock band consisting of Beths from different realities. History: They meet up by borrowing their Rick's Portal gun and traveling to an agreed upon dimension where they perform. In Rick and Morty Issue 5 backup comic, they play at The Rathole where they end up fighting the audience. Beth Smith (C-132) is seen wearing one of their shirts in Rick and Morty Issue 11.
