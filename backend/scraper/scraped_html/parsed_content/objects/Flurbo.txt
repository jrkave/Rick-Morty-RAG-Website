The flurbo (plurally flurbos) is a form of currency used by some alien species. History: In "Mortynight Run" Rick sells an antimatter gun to the assassin Krombopulos Michael for 3,000 Flurbos, with the intention of using these for Blips and Chitz. Appearances: "Mortynight Run" "Wet Kuat Amortican Summer"(mentioned) Trivia: The "Flurbo" Project is a real crypto-currency based on CryptoNote.
